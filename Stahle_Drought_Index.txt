DESCRIPTION:
This data set contains six files of reconstructed drought Index,
precipitation, river flow, and Southern Oscillation Index (SOI), 
derived from tree-ring data by Dr. D.W. Stahle and Dr. M. K. Cleaveland
at the University of Arkansas-Fayetteville and Mr. D.N. Duvick of Iowa
State University.  Descriptions and references for each data file are 
listed below.


The long series is the reconstructed July Palmer Hydrological Drought
Index from Missouri climate div. 5 (East Ozarks climate div.).  The
observed data from that division is also included from the National
Climatic Data Center (Asheville, NC) CD-ROM updated to 1992.

NOTE: Although the paper only looks at the reconstruction from 1680-on,
where the tree-ring chronologies are best replicated, the recon actually
starts in 1596.

This is the series reconstructed and analyzed in the following publication:

Cleaveland, M.K. and D.W. Stahle, 1994, Reconstruction of Ozark Highlands
paleoclimate from tree rings--applications to global change.  Presented
at the International Conf. on Tree Rings, Environment and Humanity, May
1994.
